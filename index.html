Terms of Use & Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to God's Children. By creating an account and using this app, you agree to the following terms:

1. Community Standards

God's Children is a faith-based community. We have zero tolerance for objectionable, offensive, hateful, or abusive content. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Harassment, bullying, or threats
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Sexually explicit or violent content
  • Spam or misleading content
  • Profanity or vulgar language

2. Content Moderation

Users can report objectionable content and block abusive users. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours. Offending content will be removed and the responsible user may be permanently banned.

3. User-Generated Content

You are responsible for content you post including discussions, testimonies, prayer requests, and comments. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines.

4. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from your profile settings. This will permanently remove your profile data. Some content you posted may remain but will be anonymized.

5. Privacy

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service (name, email, username). We do not sell your data to third parties.
